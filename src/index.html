Climate change the big picture

Environmental changes have certainly been a major topic for the last decades but we could argue that due to the massive amount of information that is regularly given by news, it can be hard to filter out the actual relevant facts that influence climate evolution. A lot of raw data on the matter are available but hard to interpret to get the full picture. That's the seed of our motivation behind this project, getting a full picture of environmental changes happening for last decades and awaken (the force) awareness. Therefore, this project implements a visualization of this raw data to take a better grasp at the different aspects of the actual changes.
